1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") governs how Afreximbank African Trade Center, Abuja ("we", "us", or "Bank") collects, uses, and protects personal data of visitors ("you") through our Visitor Management System (VMS) deployed at Plot 1573, Central Business District, By Ministry of Finance, Off Ralph Sodeinde Street, Abuja, Nigeria.

Key Objectives:
  • Ensure NDDPA (Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023) compliance
  • Maintain banking security standards (CBN Guidelines)
  • Provide transparency about visitor data processing
Scope

Applies to all individuals:

Visitors entering Bank premises
Employees hosting visitors
Security personnel processing check-ins
